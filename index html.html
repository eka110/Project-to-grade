What is Plasticwatcher?
Plasticwatcher is the personal initiative. It is protest driven. Founded on social enterprise core principle.
Its mission is to reduce plastic waste and pollution, to inspire people to follow mindful lifestyle and to speak up about plastic pollution.
To demonstrate circular economys best examples and promote its business processing.
Plasticwatcher knows that nearly all trash can be recycled and up cycled.
By collecting household plastic waste and expired advertising banners and roll up materials from partner organization, we create sustainable fashion items and interactive art objects.
